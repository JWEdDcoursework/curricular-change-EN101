ðŸ“‹ Purposefully Excluded Stakeholders

Students

Reason for Exclusion: While students are the ultimate beneficiaries of this change, they are purposefully excluded from the planning and implementation support group. This curricular change involves faculty-facing processes (norming sessions, rubric development, coordination protocols) that require pedagogical expertise. Student input will be gathered through course evaluations and success data analysis, but direct involvement in the planning process would not be appropriate given the professional development nature of the work.

Faculty from Other Departments

Reason for Exclusion: This initiative is specifically focused on EN101 instruction within the School of Humanities. While interdisciplinary perspectives can be valuable, expanding the support group to include faculty from other departments would dilute focus and slow decision-making. The shared resources and norming protocols being developed are discipline-specific and require English composition expertise.

SUNY System Administration

Reason for Exclusion: Although this initiative directly responds to Chancellor King's Academic Momentum Campaign goals, SUNY System administration is not included as an active support group member. The campus has autonomy to determine how to meet the 80% gateway completion target. SUNY's role is to set expectations and track progress, not to be involved in campus-level implementation strategies.
